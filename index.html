Great Zimbabwe

  • Located in Masvingo
  • Former capital of the Kingdom of Zimbabwe
  • It is recognised as a World Heritage Site byUNESCO
  • Name can be loosly translated to 'Great houses of stone'
  • Construction begun in the 11th century and ended in the 15th century
  • Constructed using dry stone (without mortar)
